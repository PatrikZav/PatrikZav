About

I am a high school student from Kroměříž, Czech Republic, passionate about programming, web development, and fitness. I’m on a mission to build a life of freedom for myself and my family through technology and discipline. With a clear vision and strong work ethic, I am committed to turning ambition into reality. I represent a new generation determined to succeed on my own terms.

Early Life

I was born on November 30, 2007, in Kroměříž, Czech Republic, where I was raised in a calm and supportive environment. Like many kids, I grew up fascinated by heroes — both fictional and real. In 2024, a curiosity sparked by gaming and online videos led me into the world of programming and web development.


I began creating digital projects and videos, many of which are now featured on my personal website. While I'm still in high school, I balance my schoolwork with regular gym sessions five times a week, building discipline both mentally and physically. Inspired by self-made individuals like Iman Gadzhi, Jan Šrajer, David Luu, and Jan Liška, I am driven by a desire for independence and a commitment to provide a better future for my family. With clear focus and strong ambition, I am steadily building the foundations of a life on my own terms.

Projects

YouTube

Founded on January 1, 2014, my YouTube channel Pazaxcz features montages from video games, car edits, and glimpses of my life, including go-kart racing. The channel combines high-energy content with personal insights, aiming to entertain and inspire viewers.

First personal web page

My first website was created after just three months of studying IT, marking a significant milestone in my journey as a web developer. Although some sections were left incomplete, it served as an important learning experience that laid the foundation for my future projects.

BMW M4 website

My BMW M4 website was created as part of a school project after seven months of studying IT. This project stands as my most sophisticated work to date, showcasing my growing skills in web development without the use of JavaScript but it is not optimized for devices other than computers.

Budget App

This project is the first one I created as part of a development team and also my first project using JavaScript. The application allows users to record and manage their income and expenses directly in the browser. It stores all data using Local Storage, so no backend connection is required. At the moment, the app is not optimized for mobile devices and is best viewed on a desktop computer. It helps users track their financial balance over time through a simple and user-friendly interface for personal finance organization.
